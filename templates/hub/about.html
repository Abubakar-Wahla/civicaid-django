About CivicAid

CivicAid is built to reduce friction between people who need help and people who can help. The goal is simple: clear requests, fast matching, and a transparent status workflow.

This portfolio project demonstrates Django fundamentals: models/ORM, forms, authentication, permissions, search, pagination, and APIs (DRF).
